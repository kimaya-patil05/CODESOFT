About Kim Tech

Technology with a human touch

Hi, I'm Kim - Founder & Lead Developer

At Kim Tech, we believe technology should serve people, not the other way around. Our approach combines technical expertise with deep understanding of human needs and behaviors.

With over 8 years of experience in the industry, I've learned that the most successful projects are those that prioritize user experience and human connection above all else.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me mentoring aspiring developers, exploring nature, or experimenting with new ways to make technology more accessible and intuitive for everyone.
